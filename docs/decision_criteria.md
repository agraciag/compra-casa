# Decision-Making Criteria for Property Selection

## Overview
A structured approach to evaluate and select the optimal property based on personal needs, financial constraints, and market conditions in Zaragoza.

## Personal Requirements Assessment

### 1. Space Requirements
- Minimum square meters needed
- Number of bedrooms required
- Number of bathrooms needed
- Additional space preferences (office, storage, etc.)

### 2. Location Preferences
- Preferred districts/neighborhoods in Zaragoza
- Commute time to work/school
- Proximity to family/friends
- Access to public transportation
- Desired neighborhood characteristics (quiet, vibrant, family-friendly)

### 3. Lifestyle Factors
- Need for parking space
- Outdoor space preferences (terrace, garden)
- Proximity to amenities (shops, restaurants, schools)
- Access to recreational facilities

## Financial Evaluation Framework

### 4. Budget Constraints
- Maximum purchase price
- Available down payment
- Acceptable monthly mortgage payment
- Additional costs budget (renovation, furnishing)

### 5. Value Assessment
- Price per square meter comparison
- Value score relative to similar properties
- Potential for appreciation
- Cost of ownership (HOA fees, utilities, maintenance)

## Property Evaluation Matrix

### 6. Scoring System
Create a weighted scoring system with categories:

#### Financial Score (40% of total)
- Price relative to market value (15%)
- Price per square meter (10%)
- Affordability ratio (10%)
- Additional costs (5%)

#### Location Score (30% of total)
- Neighborhood desirability (10%)
- Transportation access (10%)
- Proximity to amenities (5%)
- Future development potential (5%)

#### Property Quality Score (20% of total)
- Condition and age (8%)
- Layout efficiency (7%)
- Features and amenities (5%)

#### Market Conditions Score (10% of total)
- Time on market (3%)
- Price history (4%)
- Market competition (3%)

### 7. Minimum Thresholds
- Properties must meet minimum requirements in each category
- Non-negotiable criteria (location, size, etc.)
- Deal-breaker features to eliminate properties

## Decision Process

### 8. Initial Screening
- Eliminate properties that don't meet basic requirements
- Filter by budget constraints
- Remove properties in undesirable locations

### 9. Detailed Evaluation
- Apply scoring system to remaining properties
- Consider personal preferences weightings
- Evaluate long-term implications

### 10. Final Comparison
- Top 3-5 properties for detailed analysis
- Scenario planning for each option
- Risk assessment for each choice

## Due Diligence Requirements

### 11. Legal Verification
- Property title verification
- Outstanding liens or debts
- Community fees and obligations
- Urban planning compatibility

### 12. Physical Inspection
- Structural integrity assessment
- Age and condition evaluation
- Necessary repairs or renovations
- Energy efficiency rating

## Decision Timeline

### 13. Urgency Factors
- Market conditions (seller's vs buyer's market)
- Property-specific factors (multiple offers, motivated seller)
- Personal timeline constraints
- Seasonal market variations

## Alternative Scenarios

### 14. What-If Analysis
- Impact of interest rate changes
- Effect of market fluctuations
- Consequences of extending search period
- Opportunity cost of waiting vs. acting

## Final Decision Framework

### 15. Go/No-Go Criteria
- Minimum composite score threshold
- Alignment with personal requirements
- Financial feasibility confirmation
- Intuition and comfort level

### 16. Negotiation Strategy
- Based on evaluation results
- Market position of the property
- Time sensitivity factors
- Comparison with alternative options

## Post-Decision Validation

### 17. Review Process
- Document reasons for selection
- Compare with initially identified criteria
- Validate that decision meets all requirements
- Plan for implementation steps

This systematic approach ensures that property selection is based on objective criteria while accounting for personal preferences and financial constraints, leading to the best possible decision for your specific situation in the Zaragoza market.
